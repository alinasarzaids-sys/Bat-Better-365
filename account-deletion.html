Account Deletion Request

Bat Better 365 is committed to respecting your privacy and data rights. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to our support team at the address below

Email us at:

batbetter365@gmail.com

Subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Step 2: Include the following information in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Username (if applicable)
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Step 3: Wait for confirmation from our team

We will confirm your request and process the deletion within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove the following data:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password, profile details
  • Training Data: All training session records, drills completed, performance metrics, and progress tracking
  • Journal Entries: All journal entries and personal notes
  • Calendar Events: All scheduled training sessions and calendar events
  • AI Coach Conversations: All conversation history with the AI coach
  • Achievements & Progress: All earned achievements, skill levels, and streaks

⚠️ Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems. You will not be able to recover your training history, progress, or any other data. If you wish to use Bat Better 365 again in the future, you will need to create a new account and start from the beginning.

Data Retention Period

After receiving your deletion request:

  • Processing Time: Up to 30 days from the date of your request
  • Backup Systems: Data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days before permanent deletion
  • Legal Compliance: Some anonymized data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes, but will not be linked to your identity

Alternative: Reset Account Data

If you want to keep your account but remove your training data, you can use the "Reset Account" option in the app settings instead:

  1. Open Bat Better 365 app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to "Actions" section
  4. Tap "Reset Account"

This will delete your training data and progress while keeping your login credentials intact.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: batbetter365@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Account Deletion Inquiry"
  • Response Time: Within 72 hours
